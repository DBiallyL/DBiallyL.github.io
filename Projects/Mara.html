Mara

Mara is a roguelite fighting game where you play as a lost soul, attempting to escape. Players must navigate tough enemies and try to survive as they fight through levels of a dreaming world, and fight to wake up.

I worked with 3 other students to create this project for my Video Games class. I acted as Software Lead in this project, assuming responsibility for a large amount of the code and guiding others through their own programming assigments. I designed the overall object structure of our software, implemented many of the physics interactions of the player, and worked with our artist to implement sprite animations.
